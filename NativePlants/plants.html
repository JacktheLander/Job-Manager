Flower Uses in Native American Culture

Echinacea
Used by the Lakota and Cheyenne for treating infections and wounds. Known for its immune-boosting properties.
Yucca
The Navajo used Yucca root for shampoo and soap, and its flowers were consumed as food and medicine.
Wild Poppy
Used for calming effects and pain relief. Some tribes infused poppy flowers for teas.
