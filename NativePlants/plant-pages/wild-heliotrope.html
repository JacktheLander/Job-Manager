Wild Heliotrope

Phacelia distans (Phacelia crenulata?)

Tapšešte Koro

Description

A low, sprawling plant with hairy stems and small lavender flowers in tight coils. Found in open, sunny areas like grasslands and valleys

Traditional Native Uses

Crushed leaves used as a poultice for sores and swelling; also used to ease coughing or chest illnesses
